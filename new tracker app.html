Smile Food Tracker

Goal

Smile Food Tracker is an extension of the BMI checker in the previous page. The goal of the app is to provide a platform for users to keep a diary of how and what they eat, check their BMI, and be able to see and regulate the impact of food on their weight, without the pressure of watching and counting calories (as it is with a lot of food apps). Features:

  • Meal Journal
  • BMI Checker
  • Chart (daily, weekly, monthly view)
